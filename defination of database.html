data base

A database is an organized collection of structured information, or data, typically stored electronically in a very ADP system. A database is typically controlled by a management system (DBMS). Together, the info and therefore the DBMS, together with the applications that are related to them, are remarked as a database system, often shortened to merely database. Data within the foremost common sorts of databases operative today is often modeled in rows and columns in an exceedingly series of tables to create processing and data querying efficient. the info can then be easily accessed, managed, modified, updated, controlled, and arranged. Most databases use structured source language (SQL) for writing and querying data.
